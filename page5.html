Buffalo Soldiers

I chose the Black Swordsman Party from Berserk to be my personal soldiers for their ability to live in spite of their great suffering. I chose Fireteam Alpha nine from Halo ODST as my Professional soldiers due to their ability to finish a mission that had gone in almost every way during the attack on New Mombosa

Poem
In battles fierce and fights unkind, A team of soldiers you shall find, From Halo ODST, Alpha Nine, To the Black Swordsman Party, divine. Buck leads with his tactical mind, Romeo snipes, with aim refined, Mickey booms, a love for the blast, Dutch, the heavy gunner, unsurpassed. Guts, the swordsman with a massive blade, Casca, quick and agile, a deadly raid, Puck, small but mighty, with tricks galore, Isidro, young and eager, always wanting more. Odd at first, but they work as one, Respect each other's leadership, battle won, Romeo and Casca, snipe and recon, Mickey and Puck, chaos is their spawn. In peace, they train and bond, In war, they fight, and they're beyond, Dutch and Guts, the armor heavy, Romeo and Isidro, light and savvy. Thanks to Buck's connections, they're well-equipped, With armor, weapons, they're not stripped, Together they stand, through thick and thin, A team of soldiers, ready to win.
